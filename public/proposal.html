Project Overview

Client Request: The homeowners would like to have a complete remodel at their second floor bathroom. Major upgrades include increased shower area, additional storage cabinet, enlarging the overall floor space and a larger vanity.

Ensuite Bathroom Floor: approx. 13.3' x 7.7'

Main Project Tasks

Breakdown for main project items (labor-only component):

  • 1

    Material Procurement & Delivery

    We will recommend and help with transportation of all selected materials, ensuring quality and timely arrival to stay on schedule.

  • 2

    Site Preparation & Protection

    The work area and pathway will be covered with protective materials to safeguard surfaces and contain debris.

  • 3

    Shower, Drain, Vanity and Toilet Removal

    The existing shower will be detached, with careful disconnection of the drain to protect the plumbing.

  • 4

    Tile, Wall and Floor Removal

    Existing tiles, grout, and waterproofing will be removed carefully to reveal bare studs and subfloor while minimizing impact on surrounding areas.

  • 5

    Debris Removal & Cleanup

    All removed materials are properly disposed of to the disposal container outside and the work site is cleaned to prepare for the next phase.

  • 6

    Framing & Subfloor Inspection

    After removal, we will inspect the studs and subfloor for potential issues like mold or structural concerns. Additional blocking will be added.

  • 7

    Drywall Installation

    Shower walls will be leveled and plumbed for stability. Moisture-resistant drywall will be installed throughout the bathroom.

  • 8

    Custom Shower Pan and Drain Installation

    The prefabricated under-tiles shower pan will be installed to allow for optimal drainage.

  • 9

    Waterproofing

    A high-quality waterproof membrane (Schluter) will be applied to all necessary surfaces, including corners, edges, floor and wall-to-floor transition to ensure no leaks followed by a 24hrs water test.

  • 10

    Tile Layout, Cutting, and Installation

    Tiles will be measured and cut precisely, with thin-set mortar used for secure application. Shower walls and floor tiles will be installed.

  • 11

    Grouting and Sealing

    Grout will fill the gaps between tiles, creating a watertight seal. Matching color silicone is applied at all plane-changing edges for added seal and protection.

  • 12

    Shower Glass Panel and Trim Installation

    New glass panels will be installed with precise alignment and secure mounting, complemented by thoughtfully fitted shower trim and additional hardware.

  • 13

    Finishing Touches

    Joint sealing, sanding, and preparation for painting as required on both walls and ceiling.

  • 14

    Walls, Ceiling and Trim Painting

    Applying primer to ensure optimal paint adhesion. Applying 2 coats of high-quality paint to achieve even coverage and a flawless finish. Cutting in along edges, corners, and trim to ensure sharp, professional lines.

  • 15

    Toilet and Vanity Installation

    Carefully install the toilet and vanity and connect required plumbing. Seal with waterproof silicon where required.

  • 16

    Final Cleanup & Inspection

    The workspace will be professionally cleaned, and a final quality check will ensure customer satisfaction.
